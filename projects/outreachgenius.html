About Us

AI is here to help us do 2 things more easily than at any point in history - Imagine and Create. This belief is at the center of everything we are building at OutreachGenius. OutreachGenius simplifies lead generation and sales for B2B companies with precise and personalized outreach powered by AI. Our precision targeting system leverages a proprietary database of 300M+ B2B and B2C records. Our personalization AI provides real-time personalized marketing intelligence about any prospect with a LinkedIn profile to generate a 'Know Your Prospect Score Card' with insights like press mentions, podcast appearances, personality summaries and more about your lead.

Our products?

At OutreachGenius.ai, our product suite is designed to revolutionize B2B lead generation and sales with cutting-edge AI technology.
