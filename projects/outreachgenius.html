About Us

OutreachGenius is a voice AI platform that helps businesses automate personalized outbound calling and lead generation, especially in markets where traditional digital channels underperform. We focus on helping home service and franchise-style businesses reach and convert leads more effectively by replacing channels like email and Facebook ads with AI-powered phone outreach that actually gets answered.

What Makes OutreachGenius Special?

What sets OutreachGenius apart is our focus on voice-first outreach. Many leads in home service and franchise markets simply don't respond to emails or digital ads. Our AI calling technology reaches these leads directly by phone, using personalization to improve pickup rates and drive real conversations.

We automate outbound calling at scale while keeping each interaction personal and relevant. This means businesses can contact, qualify, and follow up with far more leads than a human team could handle alone, without sacrificing quality.

Our startup nature drives us to constantly innovate and adapt, ensuring that our solutions remain on the cutting edge. This ethos encourages flexibility, rapid development, and a customer-first approach to building tools that make lead generation and follow-up more scalable and efficient.

What We Do

At OutreachGenius, our platform is built to handle the full outbound pipeline. We provide AI-powered calling that automates phone-based outreach, reaching leads that don't respond well to normal channels like email or Facebook ads.

Our system handles lead qualification and follow-up automatically, ensuring no lead falls through the cracks. Each call is personalized using AI to maximize pickup rates and create meaningful conversations that convert.

The result is a scalable outbound engine that lets businesses reach, qualify, and convert leads at a volume that would be impossible with manual calling alone.

Our Values

At OutreachGenius, our core values are anchored in the belief that AI should make outreach more human, not less. We are dedicated to innovation, employing voice AI to deliver personalized conversations at scale. Our platform is purpose-built for businesses where a phone call beats a cold email — helping them deeply understand and effectively engage their prospects. This approach not only improves lead generation efficiency but also elevates every interaction through relevant, timely, and personal outreach.
