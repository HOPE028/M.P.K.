Project:

Manage

What is it?

Manage is a Web App created for teams to track the hours members are working / volunteering / practicing. Aside from offering hour tracking abilities, it offers comprehensive social media capabilities such as messaging other team members, viewing other member's information, having dynamic ways of seeing how your hours have been changing, and most importantly, having completely customizable information access and member information security.
